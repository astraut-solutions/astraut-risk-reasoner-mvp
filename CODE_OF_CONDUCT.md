# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants for violations.
